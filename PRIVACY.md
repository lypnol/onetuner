# Privacy Policy — OneTuner

**Last updated:** April 4, 2026

## What data does OneTuner collect?

None.

## Microphone access

OneTuner uses your device's microphone solely to detect the pitch of sounds in real-time. Audio is processed entirely on your device. No audio is recorded, stored, or transmitted anywhere.

## Third-party services

OneTuner does not use analytics, tracking, advertising, or any third-party services.

## Data sharing

OneTuner does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, open an issue at [github.com/lypnol/pitchtune](https://github.com/lypnol/pitchtune).
